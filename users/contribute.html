You can build a better graph platform by getting involved with Gephi. You don’t have to be a Java guru (or even know what that means!) and you don’t need to spend lots of time. Take a look at the opportunities below and feel free to ask if you have any questions.

  • Area of Interest
  • Time Available

Area of Interest

Browse contribution opportunities by area of interest.

Open Specifications

Make Gephi the killer-app you ever wanted! Gephi orientation-making is now more transparent: you can share your ideas and request more defined features.

User Support

Interested in helping others get the most out of using Gephi and other community projects? Our support process relies on enthusiastic contributors like you. Find out more about supporting Gephi, and others projects like libgexf and gexfExplorer.

Localization

Get involved by making Gephi available in your language.

Testing and Quality Assurance

QA is one of the easiest ways to get started with Gephi. Help us squashing bugs in Gephi.

Marketing

Help us spread the word about how Gephi and other community projects are building a better graph platform. There are opportunities to promote Gephi to users, developers and students.

Plugins

Gephi has a powerful extension system that allow you to make small tweaks or make major additions to your programs. If you’re new to this technology, learn how to build a plugin and request mentorship on the forum.

Coding

Since the beginning of the Gephi project the goal has always been to set up around getting code into people’s hands. Get prepared to start hacking by finding out about our code repositories, processes and tools.

User & Developer Documentation

The Gephi Wiki is the hub for information about Gephi technologies. Help make our documentation better by writing new content, expected topics, or correcting existing material.

Time Available

Only have 5 minutes? Do you want to dive deep on a big project? Browse opportunities by time commitment.

A Few Minutes

Spend five minutes a day in the Gephi support forum picking a question you know the answer to and making that user’s day.
Help make our projects better by submitting a bug report if Gephi unexpectedly quits or have some troubles.

A Few Hours

Read through our localization pages to find out how to start a new localization.
Spread the word about Gephi by writing an article on your blog, Facebook page or web site.
Pick a topic from the documentation wishlist and make the Gephi Wiki even better.

A Few Weeks Or More

Create a plugin to add a cool new feature to Gephi.
Get access to our code repository by contributing patches and becoming familiar with our review process.
